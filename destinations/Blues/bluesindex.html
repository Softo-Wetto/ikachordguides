Delta Blues, Texas Blues, Chicago Blues, Piedmont Blues, Jump Blues, British Blues,

Blues is a genre of music that originated in African American communities in the southern United States in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The music has its roots in African musical traditions, work songs, spirituals, and the folk music of white Americans of European heritage. Blues music typically features lyrics that express feelings of sadness, loss, and pain, often accompanied by simple but powerful melodies and a strong rhythmic pulse. The traditional blues sound is created by using a specific set of chords and scales, and the form often follows a 12-bar structure.
