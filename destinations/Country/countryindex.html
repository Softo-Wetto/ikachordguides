Traditional country, Bluegrass, Honky-tonk, Texas Country, Red Dirt

Country music is a genre of popular music that originated in the Southern United States in the early 20th century. It has its roots in traditional American folk music, as well as the musical traditions of Irish, Scottish, and English immigrants. Country music is characterized by its simple melodies, heartfelt lyrics, and instrumentation that typically includes acoustic guitars, fiddles, and other stringed instruments. The genre has evolved over time, incorporating influences from other musical styles such as rock, pop, and blues.
