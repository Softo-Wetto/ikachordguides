Dance-Pop

Dance-pop is a sub-genre of pop music that emerged in the 1980s and has remained popular ever since. It is characterized by its upbeat and danceable rhythms, which are often accompanied by electronic instruments and synthesizers. Dance-pop songs typically have catchy melodies and lyrics that are easy to sing along with, and they are designed to get people moving on the dance floor.
