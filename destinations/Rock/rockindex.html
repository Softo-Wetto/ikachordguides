Alternative rock, Heavy metal, Psychedelic rock, Grunge, Hard rock

Rock is a broad genre of popular music that originated in the United States in the mid-1950s and quickly spread worldwide. It is characterized by a strong beat, typically driven by electric guitars, bass, and drums, as well as a focus on the vocals and lyrics of the songs. The genre has undergone many changes and developments since its inception, leading to the emergence of numerous sub-genres within the rock umbrella. In the early days of rock, the genre was heavily influenced by rhythm and blues, country music, and gospel music.
