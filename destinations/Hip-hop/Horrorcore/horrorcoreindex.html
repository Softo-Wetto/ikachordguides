Horrorcore

Horrorcore is a subgenre of hip-hop music that focuses on horror-themed and often violent lyrics. It emerged in the late 1980s and early 1990s and is characterized by graphic descriptions of horror and violence, dark and ominous beats, and the use of horror movie samples. Horrorcore often deals with taboo subjects such as murder, suicide, and torture, and it is not for everyone due to its graphic and disturbing content. Some of the notable horrorcore artists include Insane Clown Posse, Brotha Lynch Hung, and Gravediggaz.
