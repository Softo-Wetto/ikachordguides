Trap, Drill, Boom-Bap, Memphis, Gangsta Rap, Alternative Hip-hop

Hip hop is a genre of music that originated in African American and Latinx communities in the Bronx, New York City, during the late 1970s. It is characterized by its rhythmic beats, spoken lyrics, and often socially and politically conscious themes. Hip hop culture encompasses not just music, but also dance, fashion, and visual art. Some of the most famous hip hop artists include Jay-Z, Tupac Shakur, Notorious B.I.G., Eminem, and Kendrick Lamar. Over the years, hip hop has influenced many other genres of music and has become a global cultural phenomenon.
